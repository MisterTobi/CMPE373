Equation-Based Models¶

Derivation shows us how systems change with time.

The love of Romeo and Juliet¶

When Juliet's love increases, Romeo's love decrease!! $$ \frac{dR}{dt} = aJ $$

When Romeo's love increases, Juliet's love decrease!! $$ \frac{dJ}{dt} = -bR $$

In [10]:
import numpy as np
a, b, dt = 1, 2, 0.01

R, J = [1], [-1];
time = np.arange(0, 10, dt)

for t in range(len(time)):
    dR = a * J[t] * dt
    dJ = -b * R[t] * dt
    
    new_R = R[t] + dR
    new_J = J[t] + dJ
    
    R.append(new_R)
    J.append(new_J)
    
print(len(R))
print(len(J))
print(len(time))
